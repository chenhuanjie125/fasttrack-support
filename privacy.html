FastTrack Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

This page explains what FastTrack for macOS processes on device, which permissions it uses, and what data it does not collect or share. This policy currently applies to distribution regions outside mainland China.

Effective Date 2026-04-07 Contact Email fasttrack@showleafy.com
English 中文

Overview

Product scope

FastTrack for macOS is a local menu bar productivity tool for selection translation, clipboard history, pinned slots, and quick paste workflows. This policy applies to the FastTrack app itself and does not replace the privacy terms of the Apple App Store or macOS.

What We Do Not Collect

No developer backend or tracking

  • FastTrack does not require an account and does not provide a developer-operated account system.
  • FastTrack does not run a developer backend that receives your selected text, translation results, clipboard history, or translation history.
  • FastTrack does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, tracking, profiling, or personalized advertising.
  • FastTrack does not share user data with third parties, except where Apple processes App Store purchases as part of its platform services.

Data Processed and Stored Locally

On-device data only

To provide its features, FastTrack may process or store the following data locally on your Mac:

  • Text that you actively select for translation
  • Text and images copied to the clipboard when clipboard capture is enabled
  • Local translation history for the history window
  • App preferences such as target language, interface language, shortcuts, and clipboard options
  • Local purchase and unlock state needed to control feature availability

This data is processed and stored on your device by default and is not uploaded to a developer-operated server.

Permissions and Purpose

Accessibility and Automation

  • Accessibility: used only to read the currently selected text in another app when you explicitly trigger translation.
  • Automation / Apple Events: used only to communicate with Apple’s Shortcuts Events process to run the built-in system translation shortcut.

If you deny these permissions, FastTrack can still open, but related translation features will be limited or unavailable.

Third-Party Sharing and Apple Services

Platform services only

The current version of FastTrack uses macOS system capabilities for its core translation workflow and does not integrate third-party online translation APIs. Mac App Store purchases are processed by Apple, and the developer does not receive your full payment details.

If FastTrack adds third-party analytics, crash reporting, cloud services, or online translation providers in the future, this policy and the App Store Connect privacy answers will be updated accordingly.

Retention, Deletion, and Control

How local data is handled

  • Clipboard history remains on your device until you delete entries, clear unlocked history, reach an expiration policy, or uninstall the app.
  • Translation history remains in the local app support directory until you remove the local data or uninstall the app.
  • You can disable clipboard capture in FastTrack Settings to reduce future local records.
  • You can revoke Accessibility or Automation permissions in macOS System Settings.

Children and Region

Audience and availability

FastTrack is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. This policy currently applies to distribution regions outside mainland China.

Changes to This Policy

Future updates

If FastTrack changes its data handling, third-party dependencies, or distribution scope, this page will be updated and the effective date will be revised.

Contact

Questions about privacy

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at fasttrack@showleafy.com.
