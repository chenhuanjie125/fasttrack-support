中文

FastTrack 不依赖开发者自建服务器，不创建账号，也不会把您的选中文本、翻译结果、剪贴板历史上传到开发者运营的后端服务。

如果您正在准备 Mac App Store 上架材料，可以直接使用此站点中的支持页和隐私页作为公开 URL。

English

FastTrack does not use a developer-operated backend, does not require accounts, and does not upload your selected text, translation results, or clipboard history to a developer server.

If you are preparing Mac App Store submission materials, this site provides the public support and privacy URLs for FastTrack.
